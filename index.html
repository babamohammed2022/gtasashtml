Mod Overview

GTA San Andreas Stories, also known by its alternative titles GTA San Andreas Stories: The Return of Ryder or GTA San Andreas Stories: 1987, is a fan-created mod developed by babamohammed2022. Currently in progress, it seeks to recreate the atmosphere of the classic GTA Stories games.

This mod utilizes DYOM 8.1 and focuses on Ryder’s return to Los Santos in 1987, embarking on new adventures while reconnecting with familiar faces from the past.

For further information, join our Discord community: Join Discord

Important: Some mods are not supported and could lead to issues!

  • Essential Modpack: The Widescreen mod might cause issues. It’s advised to remove it from Modloader to avoid crashes.
  • SAMP (San Andreas Multiplayer): This mod is incompatible with SAMP and could cause crashes or other unexpected behavior.
  • Animation Mods (e.g., Ragdoll Mods): These are not supported and can cause significant problems when used alongside this mod.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Where is the setting of the mod?

The mod is set in 1987 in the city of Los Santos, a place on the brink of chaos where gangs, including the Grove Street Families, battle for control.

Q2: Who is the main protagonist of the story?

The protagonist is Lance "Ryder" Wilson, a loyal member of the Grove Street Families who returns to Los Santos from Vice City to help the gang during CJ's absence.

Q3: Why did CJ leave Los Santos?

CJ, or Carl Johnson, left Los Santos after the tragic death of his brother, Brian. He went to Liberty City to cope with the loss, leaving Sweet and the others behind to handle the gang.

Q4: Who are the main antagonists in the game?

The primary antagonist is Victor "Viper" Mendez, a dangerous drug kingpin aiming to expand his empire in Los Santos, seeing the Grove Street Families as a major obstacle.

Q5: Are there multiple chapters in the storyline?

Yes, the mod is divided into three chapters, each featuring intense gang warfare and missions that push Ryder and the Grove Street Families to their limits.

Q6: Is there any word on additional content or expansions?

Yes, once the mod is finished, there are plans for downloadable content (DLC) to further enrich the storyline and features.

Q7: How do I stay updated about the progress of "Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas Stories"?

You can find updates on the official Discord server or by following the YouTube channel "cerdopalo."

Q8: Will this mod significantly change "Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas"?

Yes, the mod will overhaul many aspects of the game, including missions, characters, gameplay mechanics, and graphics, offering a fresh experience set in 1987 Los Santos.

Q9: What gangs are involved in the storyline?

The game features several gangs, such as the Grove Street Families, Ballas, La Sombra del Cuervo, Vagos, Seville Boulevard Families, Temple Drive Families, Aztecas, Russian Mafia, and Sindaccos.

Q10: Who are the Grove Street Families' allies?

The Grove Street Families are allied with the Seville Boulevard Families, Temple Drive Families, and Tommy Vercetti.

Q11: What is Ryder's history in Vice City?

Ryder worked for Tommy Vercetti in Vice City before returning to Los Santos to reconnect with the Grove Street Families and continue his role in the gang.

Q12: Are there any side missions in the mod?

Yes, the mod includes side missions like Pizza Delivery, Trash Dash, and Drug Bust Operation, with vehicles available at various locations around the map, including Idlewood and Willowfield.

System Requirements

Ready to jump into Los Santos? Here's what your PC needs to get started:

  • Operating System: Windows 7 or later
  • Processor: Dual-Core or equivalent
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Integrated Graphics
  • Storage: 3 GB of free space
  • Additional Requirements: A clean copy of GTA San Andreas, GTA SA EXE 1.0 US version, CLEO 5, CLEO+, and DYOM 8.1 (all included in the essentials pack)
